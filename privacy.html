Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Shape of Your Love ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What we collect

When you place an order, we collect:

  • Your name and the name on the order
  • Your email address
  • Your shipping address
  • Payment information (processed securely by PayPal — we never see or store your card details)

How we use your data

We use your information solely to:

  • Fulfil and ship your order
  • Send you your shape variations by email
  • Communicate with you about your order status

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data storage

Your order data is stored securely using Supabase, a GDPR-compliant database platform. Payment processing is handled by PayPal. Neither service stores your full card details.

Emails

We send transactional emails related to your order only — order confirmation, shape variations, and shipping updates. We do not send marketing emails without your explicit consent.

Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To do so, email us at hello@shapeofyourlove.com.

Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. The only external services loaded are PayPal (for payment processing) and Google Fonts (for typography).

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@shapeofyourlove.com.
